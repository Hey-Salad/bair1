The Product

Meet Bair1.

A bear that sniffs the air for you.

  • Plug in. Connect to WiFi or cellular. Done.
  • One glance. The bear's expression changes with air quality.
  • Laser precision. Sensirion SPS30 measures PM1, PM2.5, PM10.
  • Always on. SIM800L cellular — works without WiFi.
  • Dashboard + App. Deep-dive at bair1.live or on mobile.
